Ground tracks and ground stations

The plot shows satellite ground tracks projected onto longitude & latitude (not taking altitude into account). It uses an equirectangular projection.

The ground stations are coloured circles—you can drag the circles to change their location.

Scroll to zoom in and out.

Ground station views

The four plots show the position of the satellite over time under an ENU frame, relative to the specific ground station. They use a stereographic projection.

Rise and set times

The blocks show the time intervals the sattelite is visible from a specific ground station.
